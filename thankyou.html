Types of Tickets

Different tickets usually come in various types of terms, such as Saver/Value Fare, with lower prices. With this type of fare, you will get limited changes or cancellation options. When your travel dates are fixed, you must choose this type of fare. On the other hand, if you need more flexible options, then you must select the flexible fares, but they come with higher prices. However, easier changes and cancellations are available.
